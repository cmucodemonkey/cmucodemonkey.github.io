// About

I am a professional software developer and the President of the Greater Lansing User Group for .NET Developers (aka GLUGNET). A graduate of Central Michigan University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Mathematics, I enjoy returning to campus to attend athletic events, notably football and basketball. When not working I enjoy doing photography and playing various musical instruments. I currently reside in the greater Lansing, Michigan area with my wife, our two sons, and our cats.
